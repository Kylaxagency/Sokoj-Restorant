Our Story

A Table with
Intention

Sokoj was born from a conviction that a truly memorable meal is more than what arrives on the plate — it is the light, the silence between conversations, the way time slows.

Founded in 2008 in the heart of Belgrade, we set out to create a space where the rich culinary heritage of the region could be honoured with the reverence it deserves. Our kitchen draws deeply from the terroir of Serbia and the Balkans — the slow-cured meats of the Šumadija region, the mineral richness of Fruška Gora wines, wild herbs gathered from mountain meadows.

Our chef and his team do not merely cook. They translate landscape into flavour, season into memory. Every dish is the result of deliberate choice — in sourcing, in technique, in restraint. We believe that the most sophisticated gesture in cooking is knowing precisely what to leave out.

The Sokoj Difference

A Complete Experience

Every detail considered, nothing left to chance.

01

Provenance & Precision

Every ingredient is traceable to its origin. We work with fifteen local producers who share our philosophy — that quality begins long before the kitchen, in the soil, the season, and the hands that tend them.

02

Traditional Technique

Slow cooking, open fire, fermentation, and drying — we honour the ancestral methods that coaxed extraordinary depth from simple ingredients. Patience is our most-used utensil.

03

Modern Presentation

The plate is a canvas. We approach visual composition with the same rigour as flavour. Each dish arrives as a considered object — beautiful without artifice, speaking clearly before the first bite.

04

Exclusive Atmosphere

Intimacy is a design principle. We limit covers deliberately, ensuring every guest receives the full, unhurried attention of our team. The space itself — warm stone, soft candlelight, considered silence — sets the tone.

05

Curated Wine Cellar

Our sommelier has assembled over 280 references — from the emerging terroirs of the Balkans to the great estates of Burgundy and the Rhône. Each pairing is a conversation between glass and plate.

06

Unhurried Service

An evening at Sokoj is not a transaction. Our service team are trained to anticipate without intruding, to be present without performance. Time belongs to the guest.
